Conversion Tracking

Overview

Conversion tracking measures valuable customer actions such as form submissions, purchases, calls, and sign-ups. It is essential for evaluating campaign success.

---

Conversion Goals

- Lead Form Submission
- Phone Call
- Website Purchase
- Newsletter Signup

---

Tracking Tools

Google Ads Conversion Tracking

Track conversions directly within Google Ads.

Google Analytics

Monitor user behavior and conversion paths.

Google Tag Manager

Manage tracking codes efficiently.

---

Key Metrics

Metric| Description
Conversions| Total completed actions
Conversion Rate| Percentage of visitors converting
Cost Per Conversion| Cost required for one conversion
Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)| Revenue generated per ad spend

---

Tracking Process

1. Define conversion goals.
2. Install tracking tags.
3. Verify tracking functionality.
4. Monitor performance regularly.
5. Optimize campaigns based on data.

---

Benefits

- Accurate campaign measurement
- Better optimization decisions
- Improved ROI analysis
- Enhanced marketing performance

---

Conclusion

Conversion tracking provides valuable insights that help improve campaign effectiveness and achieve business objectives.
