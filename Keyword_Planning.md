Keyword Planning

Overview

Keyword planning is the foundation of every successful Google Ads campaign. Selecting the right keywords helps reach the target audience and improves campaign performance.

---

Campaign Goal

Generate high-quality leads and increase website traffic through targeted advertising.

---

Primary Keywords

Keyword| Match Type| Search Intent

SEO Services| Phrase Match| Commercial
Google Ads Management| Exact Match| Transactional
Social Media Marketing| Phrase Match| Commercial

Long-Tail Keywords

---

Negative Keywords

To avoid irrelevant traffic:

- Free
- Jobs
- Internship
- Course
- Training

---

Keyword Strategy

High Intent Keywords

Focus on keywords with strong buying intent.

Local Keywords

Target location-based searches for better conversions.

Branded Keywords

Include brand-specific terms where applicable.

---

Expected Results

- Higher Click-Through Rate (CTR)
- Improved Quality Score
- Better Conversion Rate
- Reduced Cost Per Click (CPC)

---

Conclusion

Effective keyword planning ensures that advertisements reach the most relevant audience and maximize campaign performance.
