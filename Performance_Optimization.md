Performance Optimization

Overview

Performance optimization focuses on improving campaign efficiency, increasing conversions, and reducing advertising costs.

---

Optimization Goals

- Increase Click-Through Rate (CTR)
- Improve Conversion Rate
- Reduce Cost Per Click (CPC)
- Maximize Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)

---

Keyword Optimization

Actions

- Pause low-performing keywords.
- Increase bids for high-converting keywords.
- Add negative keywords regularly.
- Expand keyword opportunities.

---

Ad Optimization

Best Practices

- Test multiple ad variations.
- Improve headlines and descriptions.
- Use strong call-to-actions.
- Update ads based on performance data.

---

Landing Page Optimization

Focus Areas

- Faster page loading speed.
- Mobile responsiveness.
- Clear call-to-action buttons.
- Better user experience.

---

Performance Metrics

Metric| Target
CTR| 5%+
Conversion Rate| 3%+
Quality Score| 7+
ROAS| 4:1

---

Conclusion

Continuous optimization helps maximize campaign performance and improve advertising profitability.
